## Community builds and forks

The repository grants a limited permission to redistribute the unmodified
Community-configuration build from its `ShuoCommunity` scheme as **Shuo
Community**, with the source-included icon,
solely to identify its origin. The build must retain its community bundle and
storage identities, remain clearly labeled as unofficial, link to the
corresponding source, preserve this policy, and not use the official update
feed or signing material. This permission does not extend to merchandise,
services, domains, social accounts, or a materially modified product.

A modified build distributed by anyone other than the Shuo maintainers must be
clearly identified as a community fork. Unless the maintainers give written
permission, distributors must use their own product name, application icon,
bundle identifier, signing identity, update feed, and Keychain service
identifiers. They must not reuse official signing material or present their
package as an official Shuo download.

Unmodified redistribution of an official, cryptographically identical Shuo
release may retain the marks if it is clearly described as an unmodified copy,
subject to applicable law. Do not remove signatures, notarization tickets, or
release provenance and still describe the result as official.
